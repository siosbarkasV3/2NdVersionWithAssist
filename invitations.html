Tournament Invitations

Official invitation and registration information

EGCA European 2025 Goalball Tournament

We are honored to invite your national goalball team to participate in the EGCA European 2025 Goalball Tournament, the premier goalball competition in Europe.

This tournament will feature:

  • Elite Competition: Top European teams competing for continental supremacy
  • World-Class Facilities: State-of-the-art sports arena with full accessibility
  • Professional Organization: IBSA-certified officials and comprehensive support services
  • Cultural Exchange: Opportunities for international friendship and understanding
  • Media Coverage: Extensive broadcasting and digital coverage across Europe

Tournament Details & Contact

Date: March 15-20, 2025
Venue: Thessaloniki Sports Arena, Greece
Organizer: European Goalball Confederation of Athletes (EGCA)
Contact: info@egca2025.eu | +30 231 123 4567

Registration Information

Required Documents

  • Team registration form
  • Player classification certificates
  • Medical clearance forms
  • Travel documentation
  • Insurance certificates

Important Dates

  • Jan 15, 2025: Registration opens
  • Feb 15, 2025: Early registration deadline
  • Feb 28, 2025: Final registration deadline
  • Mar 10, 2025: Team arrival
  • Mar 15, 2025: Tournament begins

Participation

  • Maximum 6 players per team
  • Minimum 3 players per team
  • 1 team coach required
  • Optional team manager
  • Medical support staff welcome

Awards & Recognition

Championship Trophy

First place team receives the EGCA European Championship Trophy and gold medals

Runner-up Medal

Second place team receives silver medals and official recognition

Third Place Medal

Third place team receives bronze medals and certificates

Special Awards

Fair Play Award, Best Player, and other special recognitions

About the Tournament

Learn about our mission, goalball sport, and commitment to inclusivity

Our Mission

The EGCA European 2025 Goalball Tournament represents more than just a sporting event. We are committed to celebrating the extraordinary abilities of adaptive athletes, fostering international unity, and promoting the inclusive spirit of goalball across Europe.

Our mission is to provide a world-class platform where teams can compete at the highest level while inspiring communities and breaking down barriers through sport.

What is Goalball?

Goalball is a team sport designed specifically for athletes with visual impairments. Created in 1946 for World War II veterans, it has evolved into one of the most popular Paralympic sports worldwide.

The game is played by two teams of three players each on a volleyball-sized court. Players wear eyeshades to ensure fairness, and the ball contains bells to help players track its movement through sound.

The objective is simple yet challenging: score goals by rolling the ball past the opposing team's goal line while defending your own goal through precise positioning and quick reflexes.
